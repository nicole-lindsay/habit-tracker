    Welcome to HABITracker!

  1. Once you've entered your username, go ahead and start adding habits(good or bad) in the column on the left.
  2. The plus button next to your habit will increase the amount in the progress bar every time you press it. The minus button will decrease the amount in the progress bar every time you press it. The trash button will delete the habit entirely. This does not work like a reset button, pressing this does not affect the progress bar.
  3. On the right, there are some rewards for filling up the progress bar. As you fill the bar, you'll be able to redeem rewards with higher value.
  4. Lastly, have fun and remember that it only takes 21 days to make or break a habit!
